PUMP OPRATOR CUM MECHANIC




Pump operator ITI course (or) ITI Pump Operator cum Mechanic Course is a full-time 1 year Industrial Training certificate program The basic eligibility required to pursue this ITI pump operator cum mechanic course is passing class 10th examination with 50% marks in science and maths as the main subject. The admission process is based on the candidateâ€™s academic performance in the previous qualifying examination.After completing the ITI Pump Operator cum Mechanic course, the candidate can opt to go for wage employment or business. Pump Service Mechanic, Pump operator, Pump Fitter, Dealers service mechanic are some of the popular job roles. In case, a candidate willing to pursue further education after completing the ITI Pump Operator cum Mechanic course, he/she can choose an apprenticeship training program from an Industrial Training Institute.



Your Future Scope in pump operator cum mechanic.


  • Through the course, the candidates are provided in-depth knowledge in terms of Pump Mechanical including repairing pumps of machinery, misalignment of the pump shaft, slippage of the drive belt, worn bearings or need for lubrication, dismantle pumps and performs repairs, using hand-tools, etc
  • pump fitter
  • pump service mechanic
  • pump operator
  • spare part sales assistant/ manufacturers representative
